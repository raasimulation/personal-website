Rasim Bayramov Email  Instagram   Resume They find comfort in the discovery of the unknown. Inviting others to connect with it, their work uses the human body as an interface for a link. A link that goes through bodies of nature, objects of use, everyday experiences, technology and memory. The manifestation of these ideas find themselves in websites, installations, print, videos, performances and sculptures. Rasim Bayramov is a designer and artist interested in unfolding of objects, humans, technologies and their invisible links through websites, sculptures, print and more

From Baku to Istanbul to (currently) Richmond.
