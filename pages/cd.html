Don't Hold The Ladder Horizontally
Book Design, Publication2024
Don't Hold The Ladder Horizontally is an anthology book that archives writings, research and collaborative notes from first-year VCUarts Graphic Design MFA students. Writings of personal practice, reflections connect through inserts that contain feedback notes from the previous person to the next.
