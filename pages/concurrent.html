Concurrent Vol. 1
Print, Publication2024
Concurrent is a collaborative publication initiative with Richmond Young Writers to bring pieces to print. 6 young writers, 6 designers, 1 volume. A collaboration between RYW and VCUarts Graphic Design Graduate Students. Each of us designed a spread for a writer.

Designed and printed by me, aya khalife, Molly Garrett, Tariye George-Phillips, Nneoma Njoku, Irene Piazza.
Organized by Luiza Dale and Elise Steele.
