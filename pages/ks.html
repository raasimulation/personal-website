Kunsthall Stavanger: MC Toolkit
Website, Identity2025
Visit the website here.
Mobilizing Citizenship (MC) was an educational and research project held at Kunsthall Stavanger between 2018 and 2024, which invited both Norwegian and international artists as well as designers to collaborate with young people aged 12 to 16. This toolkit captures the knowledge, experiences, and reflections developed over the course of the project.
