Lovetoread Italic
Typeface Design2024
Lovetoread Italic is reiterating Poligraphmash's Literaturnaya, a typeface created during the Soviet Union, through sub-cultural lens of "qiji" movement. This typeface cuts through Liteturnaya and creates space for online Azerbaijani emo culture to fit in. Special characters are made from symbols often used in Odnoklassniki and Facebook.
