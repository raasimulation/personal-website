Polyphonic Shimmering is a collection of writing that shimmers and hums, playing with duration and pacing and expanding language beyond its conventional encasements. The 12 pieces that make up the publication allow us to see what their authors see, both visual and textual, but mostly through language that blurs the distinction between the two.
