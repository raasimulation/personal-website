what i found under the tree
Installation, Website2023
Visit the project
"what i found under tree" (2023) reimagined themes of queer archive. The private corner facilitates an invitation to glance through someone's private computer and find diary entries from an unspecified individual about the trees and the sea. Specific contextual clues, from tree types in past neighborhoods to online collective representation of the Caspian sea carry themselves throughout the chapters of the pseudo-native experience. The experience doesn't reward the person with the secrets of the diary but instead expects an exchange of input, be it text, drawing or sound, in order to open up and share.
